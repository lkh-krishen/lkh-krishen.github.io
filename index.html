Intelligent Defense System for Identifying, Predicting and Mitigating Terrorist Activities

Why Should You Use Terror Scan?

Reconnaissance is a crucial part of military combat; as well as attack prediction and develop necessary mitigation and combat strategies to fight back or retreat. Simultaneously communicating this information among the troops is pivotal to saving lives. The advancement of drone technology has opened up new possibilities for monitoring and combating terrorist activities. In this research study, the objective is utilizing image data obtained through drone monitoring to support the prediction of target variables related to terrorist attacks, specifically concentrating on the prediction of attack types. And depending on that data predicting and developing necessary defense strategies. And finally, the predicted strategies will be communicated securely among the friendly allies and troops. The system is anchored upon developing the system cost effective and more budgetary feasible manner, focusing on developing countries suffering from a variety of wars.

Literature Survey

    The ability to extract meaningful information from the vast amount of data obtained through drone monitoring is critical in aiding counter terrorism efforts. Image data captured by drones provides valuable visual insights that can be analyzed to identify patterns, detect relevant features, and predict attack types. By leveraging image processing techniques, machine learning algorithms, and secure communication methods, we aim to develop a comprehensive system that integrates data analysis , prediction, and decision-making capabilities.
    Predicting terrorist activities has gained increasing attention in recent years as a means of averting potential violence and safeguarding communities. While much of the previous research has focused on the retrospective analysis of terrorist incidents, the transition towards predictive modeling is an essential shift in counterterrorism efforts. The study of Python et al. (Year) represents one of the pioneering works in this direction. Python et al. applied machine learning techniques to predict terrorist attacks by leveraging historical data from the Global Terrorism Database (GTD). Their approach incorporated geographic and socioeconomic features, aiming to forecast attacks at discrete spatiotemporal intervals. This research contributed to the understanding that machine learning can be a valuable tool in counterterrorism, capable of generating predictive models to preempt acts of violence.
    The prediction from the previous phase will highlight the type of attack and the severity of threat. Then based on the prediction, the model will suggest a suitable countermeasure for that threat. The prediction model of the system can be broken down into several sub-components, each contributing to the main objective of proposing suitable defenses for predicted terrorist attacks.
    In addition to the data analysis and prediction components, the research project incorporates the implementation of secure communication methods. Ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of the transmitted data between the drones, machine learning systems, and decision-making systems is of paramount importance. Robust encryption, authentication protocols, and secure channels are employed to safeguard the sensitive information exchanged within the system.

Research Objectives

  • Main Objective

    • To detect and prevent terrorist attacks by leveraging the power of AI/ML models to identify potential threats and predict where the terrorists are likely to attack. The system aims to propose suitable defense mechanisms for the predicted attack locations and transmit the defense solutions to ally troops and outposts using secure communication channels. The ultimate goal is to minimize the damage caused by terrorist attacks and protect lives and property.

  • Sub Objectives

    • In order to achieve the main functionality of the project, certain sub-objectives can be drawn out in order to fulfill the project's requirements. These sub-objectives are as follows: Detect potential terrorist threats using aerial footage by accurately identifying and tracking objects in the imagery. Identification of the characteristics of a domestic terrorist attack that behave in a predictable manner and their corresponding factors that influence the predictable behavior of the identified characteristics. Building an application that houses the infrastructure for predicting the outcomes of the identified characteristics of a domestic terrorist attack. Propose a suitable defense strategy once the potential attack areas and methods are identified. Transmit predicted Intelligent details to the outposts and friendly troops preserving confidentiality and integrity of the data.

Methodology

    Detect terrorist troops and vehicles. Analyze movements. Analyze high risk images. Adding data to the database.
    Identify a reliable data source to acquire records of details of historical domestic terrorist attacks. Analyze the data source to identify characteristics that behave in a predictable manner based on other characteristics Do data preprocessing, as necessary. Identify, then train and test suitable machine learning algorithms for each set of target variable and features. Implement server-side logic that uses the machine learning algorithms to compute the value of the target variable as an output based on the features as inputs. Implement an interface that takes inputs for features for each target variable and outputs the predicted value Perform unit tests. Integrate component with overall system. Perform integration and system tests.
    Propose a suitable defense strategy once the potential attack areas and methods are identified. Identify available resources. Deploy resources effectively. Identify pre and post. Attack strategies. Making critical decisions.
    Create a Mobile Ad-Hoc Network. Implement a Trust Based Authentication mechanism. Implement authentication mechanism to prevent MAC spoofing. Create a peer-to-peer chat application. Communicate intelligent data to outposts & troops.
